Avocado Bread

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 45-50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 C flour
  • 1.5 TSP baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Begin by peeling the avocados and discarding the seeds. Mash up the flesh as much as possible prior to the next step.
  3. Cream the avocados with 3/4 C sugar, as well as 3 eggs. Set aside while preparing the dry ingredients.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine 2 C flour and 1.4 TSP baking powder.
  5. Fold the dry and wet ingredients together, making sure there are no dry spots.
  6. Pour mixture into a loaf pan, and bake for 45-50 minutes.
